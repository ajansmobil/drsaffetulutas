Face and Neck Lift

Face and neck lift surgeries rejuvenate the facial oval by removing the sagging caused by the relaxation of skin and subcutaneous tissues. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş analyzes the SMAS layer, fat tissue distribution, and movements of mimic muscles in facelift planning. In this way, results that preserve mimics without a mask-like look are obtained.

In the surgery, the skin is lifted with incisions made along the ear circumference and hair line, the underlying SMAS layer is tightened, and excess tissue is removed. In the neck area, the platysma muscle is recovered, and fat accumulations in the jowl area are removed with liposuction. Dr. Ulutaş provides an aesthetic appearance by hiding the marks in the natural hair line.

The use of drains in the recovery period helps bruises and swellings to decrease rapidly. Patients are offered supportive suggestions such as rest in the first week, sleeping with a high pillow, and facial massage. While it is possible to return to social life within about two weeks, the final result clarifies in three months.

Facelift operation provides a more holistic rejuvenation by being combined with lip plumping, eyelid aesthetics, or fat injections. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş handles the face form in three regions and makes planning for each region to look consistent with each other. Thus, non-artificial, time-defying results are obtained.

If you are looking for a reliable center for face and neck lift surgery in Gaziantep, our clinic is with you with sterile operating room conditions, an experienced anesthesia team, and personalized recovery protocols. We gently rewind time on your face by combining the art aspect of aesthetic surgery with scientific discipline.
