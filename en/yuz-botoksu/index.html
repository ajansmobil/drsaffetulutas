Facial Botox

Facial botox is applied to reduce dynamic wrinkles such as forehead lines, between the eyebrows (glabellar lines), and crow's feet. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş makes dose adjustments by measuring the mimic power of each patient; details such as eyebrow position and eyelid drooping are evaluated. Thus, natural mimics are preserved, and facial expression does not become dull.

Application time is 10-15 minutes and comfort is provided with topical anesthetic cream. Needle entry points are cleaned with antiseptic solution. Botox is precisely injected into the muscle and the wrinkle appearance starts to soften within a few days. Patients should not massage for the first 24 hours, should not keep their heads in a tilted position, and avoid heavy exercise.

When facial botox is applied regularly, collagen loss slows down and the skin looks smoother. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş treats the facial proportions of his patients holistically; he makes combined planning with filler, mesotherapy, or laser applications when necessary. Thus, the upper, middle, and lower regions of the face rejuvenate harmoniously.

After botox application, results last 4-6 months. Repeated sessions are planned when its effect starts to decrease. Dr. Ulutaş pays attention to preserving the natural facial expression by avoiding excessive dose use. This approach ensures that patients receive positive feedback from their surroundings.

For those who want to have facial botox in Gaziantep, Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş clinic serves with safe products and expert hands. You can take an appointment to say goodbye to wrinkles and gain a fresh expression.
