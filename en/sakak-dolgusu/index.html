Temple Filler

Temple filler aims to rebalance the upper part of the face by filling the hollows that occur with age in the temporal region. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş takes the vascular map into account and performs injections with safe cannula techniques in this application. It is an effective method for the upper face to receive a slight lifting effect and to support the outer parts of the eyebrows.

Fillers with hyaluronic acid or calcium hydroxyapatite contents are preferred. Injection depth is adjusted in a controlled manner; thus fluctuation does not occur on the surface. To reduce the risk of intravascular injection during the procedure, suction technique and the use of cannulas are made mandatory.

There may be a slight pressure feeling after temple filler; it disappears in a few days. Patients should not put pressure on their faces during sleep and avoid activities such as hot showers and saunas for the first day. The effect of the filler lasts 12-24 months, and maintenance sessions are planned according to the person.

Filling the temporal region provides the under-eye and cheekbone region to look fuller, supporting the V-shape of the face. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş achieves a result that acts as a bridge between temple filler and eyebrow lifting operation. Thus, the upper face is rejuvenated without the need for surgery.

For temple filler application in Gaziantep, our clinic provides services with an experienced team and safe techniques. You can get an appointment to eliminate the feeling of emptiness in your upper face and gain a more vibrant expression.
