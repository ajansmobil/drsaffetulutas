Nasolabial Filler

Nasolabial filler is applied to soften deep lines between the nose wing and the corner of the mouth and show the face younger. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş evaluates the cheek support, muscle tone around the mouth, and skin elasticity when evaluating the factors causing volume loss in this area. If necessary, combined applications are planned with cheek or chin fillers.

Hyaluronic acid-based fillers are injected along the nasolabial trough with micro-cannula technique. The amount of filler is kept controlled; since artificial appearance may occur if excessive volume is given, natural folds are preserved. Dr. Ulutaş also improves smile aesthetics by supporting the corners of the mouth.

Anesthetic cream is applied before the process, and it takes about 15 minutes. Light swelling or sensitivity may occur afterward; recovery speeds up with cold compress and hygiene rules. The permanence of the filler varies between 12-18 months. Patients are recommended to have regular skin care and protection from the sun.

Nasolabial filler reduces tired and sad appearance by softening facial expressions. Fillers used in our clinic provide a lift effect and support collagen production. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş evaluates the facial proportions of his patients collectively and makes personalized planning.

For those who want to have nasolabial filler in Gaziantep, our clinic offers expert doctor support and quality product guarantee. You can confidently create an appointment for a younger and more vivid expression.
