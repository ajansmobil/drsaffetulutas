Breast Reconstruction

Breast reconstruction is an advanced reconstructive surgical procedure performed to recreate breast tissue after mastectomy or trauma. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş takes both health and aesthetic goals of the patient into account by working in coordination with breast oncology specialists in this process. The reconstruction option can be performed in the same session with mastectomy, or planned after chemotherapy and radiotherapy processes.

Methods used include silicone implants, tissue expanders, and flaps taken from the patient's own tissue (DIEP, latissimus dorsi, etc.). Which method will be preferred is determined according to the tissue quality, vascular structure, general health status, and personal request of the patient. Mastopexy or reduction can be applied to the healthy breast to preserve symmetry.

The reconstruction process is not only a surgical procedure; it requires psychological support, physiotherapy, and long-term follow-up. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş team closely monitors wound healing, the risk of lymphedema that may occur in the arm, and prosthesis harmony in the post-operative period. If necessary, nipple reconstruction and areola pigmentation are performed in the second stage.

Personalized exercise plans are prepared to facilitate the return of patients to their daily lives. Both aesthetic results and health status are followed with supportive underwear, sleep position recommendations, and regular checks. Patient privacy and comfort are prioritized at every stage of the reconstruction.

For women who want to have breast reconstruction in Gaziantep, Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş clinic is a reassuring companion. The experienced team, understanding the difficulties experienced during the oncological treatment process, reshapes the lost breast tissue with art and science. In this way, you can take aesthetically and emotionally strong steps to a new beginning.
