Our Privacy Policy

In the clinic of Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş, the personal and medical data of our patients are protected according to the highest privacy standards. Information in appointment, communication, and consultation forms collected through our website is used solely for the purpose of providing the requested service. Data is kept on encrypted servers, and explicit consent is obtained from the patient before being shared with third parties.

Medical documents held in physical archives are protected with security locks and alarm systems against unauthorized access. Surgery photos, videos, and 3D simulation records are shared only between the doctor and the patient; written permission must be obtained before being used for promotional purposes. Our clinic fully implements the technical and administrative measures required by KVKK and all relevant legislation.

The privacy of our online visitors is also important. Our cookie policy collects only anonymous data to improve user experience and provide a secure session. Users can change their cookie preferences at any time through browser settings; when cookie use is rejected, the basic functions of the site will still continue to work.

Our patients have the right to request access, update, deletion, or restriction of the processing of their personal data. These rights are finalized in a short time with a written application. Our privacy policy is reviewed at regular intervals and updated in accordance with legal regulations. The importance we give to your privacy makes Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş clinic a reliable health destination.
