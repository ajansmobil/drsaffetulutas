About Our Clinic

Founded by Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş, a Specialist in Plastic, Aesthetic, and Reconstructive Surgery, our clinic offers aesthetic solutions in Gaziantep at international standards. All our processes are built on scientific updates, sterile working protocols, and a personalized planning approach.

Our Founder

Dr. Ulutaş completed his education at Gaziantep University Faculty of Medicine and finished his compulsory service at Cizre State Hospital in 2016. The experience he gained at Dr. Ersin Arslan Training and Research Hospital forms the basis of the high-precision surgical techniques we apply in our clinic today.

Our Approach

We evaluate the needs of each of our clients individually and design plans that will preserve natural facial and body proportions. Pre-operative information, post-operative follow-up, and recovery management are supported by our multidisciplinary team.

Our Values

  • Ethical and transparent communication
  • Safe surgical techniques and sterilization
  • Using up-to-date technologies and continuous education
  • Unconditional respect for patient privacy

In our clinic in Gaziantep, we provide service by believing that aesthetic surgery is a combination of art and science.
