Arm Lift (Brachioplasty)

Arm lift surgery is performed to recover the saggings that occur in the inner arm due to weight changes or aging. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş evaluates skin elasticity, the amount of fat tissue, and the patient's expectations in brachioplasty planning. If necessary, both skin excess and fat accumulation are removed in the same session by being combined with liposuction.

During the operation, excess skin is removed with incisions made from the inner line of the arm, muscle tissues are supported and the new contour is fixed with sutures. Dr. Ulutaş pays attention that the scars remain on the inner surface of the arm; thus even when you lift your arm, minimum visibility is provided. Bleeding control and drain use are meticulously managed for the patient's rapid return to daily activities.

In the post-operative period, the arm area is supported with elastic bandages or special corsets. Keeping the arms above heart level in the first days reduces edema. Silicone-based creams and lymph drainage massages are recommended for the healing of the suture sites. Patients can return to desk jobs in 7-10 days, but it is necessary to wait six weeks for heavy sports.

Arm lift may not be enough alone for a fit silhouette; for this reason, Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş protects body integrity with combined planning with tummy tuck, liposuction, or breast aesthetics. The results are ensured to be long-lasting by reviewing the nutrition program and exercise habits of the patients.

Anyone considering arm lift surgery in Gaziantep can experience a safe and sterile surgical experience suitable for their personal goals in our clinic. An operation entrusted to expert hands and correct planning helps you leave behind the feeling of looseness in your arms.
