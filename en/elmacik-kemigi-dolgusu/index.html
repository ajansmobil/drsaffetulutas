Cheekbone Filler

Cheekbone filler aims to make the face look young and dynamic by adding volume to the midface area. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş analyzes the height of the cheekbone prominence, nasolabial groove depth, and under-eye gaps in this application. The goal is to support the facial triangle and achieve natural contours.

Hyaluronic acid-based fillers are injected onto the cheekbone and onto the lateral line of the cheek with a micro-cannula. High-density fillers provide structural support, while more fluid formulas increase superficial smoothness. Op. Dr. Ulutaş meticulously adjusts the injection depth both to create a lifting effect and prevent a heavy appearance.

Anesthetic cream is applied before the process, the application time is approximately 15 minutes. Mild edema can be seen afterwards, but the return to daily activities is fast. The persistence of the filler varies between 12-18 months; skin care and lifestyle can affect this period. Facial symmetry is reviewed at follow-up appointments and minor additions are made if needed.

Cheekbone filler reduces the depth of nasolabial lines, gives support to the under-eye area, and makes the jawline more prominent. Rejuvenation is provided in the middle area of the face by restructuring the fat pads lost with aging. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş observes a harmony consistent with other parts of the face while planning cheekbone filler.

For those who want to have cheekbone filler in Gaziantep, our clinic offers natural and long-lasting results. You can get an appointment from our expert team to increase the light on your face and gain a dynamic expression.
