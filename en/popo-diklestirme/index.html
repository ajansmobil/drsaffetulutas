Butt Lift and Contouring

Butt lift operations aim to provide a young and dynamic look to the gluteal area that has sagged or lost volume. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş creates personalized lifting plans by evaluating the hip structure, waist curve, and thigh proportions of his patients. The most correct option is determined among fat transfer, placement of silicone prosthesis, or surgical lift techniques.

With the fat transfer method known as Brazilian Butt Lift (BBL), fat cells taken from the waist, lower abdomen, and thigh areas are injected into the butt area after undergoing special processes. In this way, both the waist thins and the butt looks fuller. In cases containing excess skin, sagged tissues are removed with surgical lift and the gluteal area is repositioned.

In the post-operative period, it is recommended for patients to lie face down for a while, limit their sitting times and use special support cushions. While light walks support blood circulation, the use of a corset helps the new contour to be permanent. Dr. Ulutaş offers nutrition and movement suggestions to increase the cell attachment rate in patients who have undergone fat injection.

Butt aesthetics not only provides fullness; it differentiates waist curves, shows legs longer and provides freedom in choosing clothes. Liposuction, tummy tuck, or thigh lift operations can be applied in the same session with combined planning. Thus, a balanced silhouette is obtained in the entire lower body area.

For those considering butt lift and contouring in Gaziantep, Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş clinic offers advanced surgical techniques, a sterile environment, and the advantage of close follow-up. You can meet with our expert team for natural-looking, permanent, and safe results, and approach the hip form you dream of with confident steps.
