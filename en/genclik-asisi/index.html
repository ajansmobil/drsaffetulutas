Youth Vaccine

Youth vaccine is a treatment where injections containing hyaluronic acid, amino acids, vitamins, and minerals are applied together to intensely moisturize the skin, increase elasticity, and reduce fine wrinkles. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş determines the number of courses and content combination by analyzing the skin structure of his patients. The face, neck, cleavage, and top of the hands are among the most frequently preferred areas.

The application is performed with micro-needles or with a mesotherapy gun. Ingredients distributed homogeneously on the skin surface trigger collagen and elastin production in the dermal layer. Comfort is provided by applying anesthetic cream before the procedure; mild redness can be seen after the sessions that last approximately 20-30 minutes.

The youth vaccine course is generally applied in 2-4 sessions with three or four-week intervals. Results begin to be seen from the first session; the skin looks brighter and fuller. A maintenance session is recommended once or twice a year for the effect to be permanent. Dr. Ulutaş supports the treatment by also organizing the content of skin care products.

This treatment gives more effective results when combined with procedures such as laser or chemical peeling. It is an ideal option for skins worn out due to sun damage, smoking, or intense stress. Cocktails used in our clinic are prepared in a sterile environment and follow-up is done with photos after each session.
