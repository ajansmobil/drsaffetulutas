Liposuction and Body Contouring

Liposuction is a surgical procedure aimed at reshaping body lines by reducing regional fat. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş decides which technique to use for liposuction by analyzing the body type, skin quality, and metabolic status of his patients. The most appropriate method for the person's needs is selected from VASER, laser-assisted, or power-assisted systems.

During the operation, fat tissue is broken down with small cannulas under the skin and taken out with a vacuum system. The fat obtained can be used for filling purposes in the buttocks or face area if necessary. Op. Dr. Ulutaş sees liposuction as an aesthetic modeling tool; rather than excessive fat removal, balancing body contours is aimed.

The use of a corset in the post-operative period ensures smooth healing of the skin surface. Lymph drainage massages, ultrasonic therapy, and nutrition programs reduce edema and support skin firmness. Patients can start light walks within a few days, but should wait 4-6 weeks for intense sports activities.

Liposuction is not a weight loss method alone; it gives the most effective result in patients close to their ideal weight. When combined with procedures such as tummy tuck, breast aesthetics, or butt lifting, a visible harmony is provided in body proportions. Dr. Ulutaş guides his patients throughout the process with pre- and post-operative photos.

If you are considering liposuction in Gaziantep, Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş clinic offers a safe experience with an expert surgical team and sterile operating room conditions. Your new body form can be preserved for a long time with correct planning, regular follow-up, and disciplined living habits.
