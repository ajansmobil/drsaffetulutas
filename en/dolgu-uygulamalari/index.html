Filler Applications

Hyaluronic acid-based filler applications are non-surgical aesthetic methods used to restore the volume lost with age, emphasize facial contours, and eliminate wrinkles. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş aims to achieve rejuvenating results far from exaggeration by staying true to the 'golden ratio' principles of the face and natural anatomical lines in filler procedures.

Depressions that occur over time, nasolabial lines (nose-mouth edge), cheekbone definition, lip filler, and jawline filler are the most preferred areas. Fillers also provide a fresher and more vibrant appearance by increasing the moisture capacity of the skin. Dr. Ulutaş minimizes the risk of bruising by using high-quality, FDA-approved products and micro-cannula techniques in the procedures.

The application process takes approximately 15-30 minutes and its effect starts to be observed immediately. Thanks to fillers containing local anesthetics, the procedure is extremely comfortable. Filler applications planned with personalized analyses in our clinic in Gaziantep allow you to quickly return to social life by erasing the tired expression on your face.
