Lip Filler

Lip filler is a popular aesthetic procedure performed to increase lip volume, highlight the contour line, and balance the lip-tooth ratio. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş pays attention to the Cupid's bow, philtrum lines, lip corner height, and lip thickness ratios while evaluating lip anatomy. In this way, a result far from exaggeration that preserves natural expressions is obtained.
