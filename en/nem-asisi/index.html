Moisture Injectable (Skin Booster)

Moisture injectable is a micro-injection treatment that provides brightness, flexibility, and smoothness by increasing the water retention capacity of the skin. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş measures the moisture rate with skin analysis devices and prepares cocktails supported by hyaluronic acid, amino acids, and peptides according to need. Face, neck, cleavage, and hands are the areas that benefit most from the treatment.

Superficial mesotherapy technique is used during the process; contents that give intense moisture to the dermis layer with micro-needles are left. Comfort is provided with anesthetic cream; the session takes about 20 minutes. Light redness after the application disappears within a few hours.

Moisture injectable cures are generally planned as three sessions with 2-3 week intervals. Vitality and brightening are observed on the skin after the first session; in regular application, fine lines decrease and makeup looks smoother. Dr. Ulutaş determined the products to be used at home individually to protect the effect of the treatment.

When this application is combined with processes such as laser, radiofrequency, or carbon peeling, results providing deep skin renewal are obtained. Moisture injectable makes a big difference in dry, matte, or skin exposed to cigarette smoke. Cocktails applied in a sterile environment in our clinic strengthen the natural barrier of the skin.
