Tummy Tuck (Abdominoplasty)

Tummy tuck surgery is a comprehensive aesthetic surgical procedure performed to recover the abdominal area that has sagged due to childbirth, weight changes, or genetic reasons. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş considers skin thickness, abdominal muscle separation, waist curve, and the patient's weight goals when planning abdominoplasty. Blood tests are performed before the operation, and counseling is given for the regulation of habits that affect healing, such as smoking.

In the surgical stage, excess skin and fat tissue below the belly button are removed; abdominal muscles are sutured to tighten the abdominal wall. The belly button is repositioned according to the anatomy. In some cases, the hourglass appearance is supported by thinning the waistline with liposuction. Dr. Ulutaş plans the incision line below the underwear line, ensuring that the scars remain hidden.

The use of an abdominal corset in the post-operative period is critical for the rapid resolution of edema and for the body shape to become permanent. Patients should support circulation with light walks in the first week and at the same time rest in the position recommended by the doctor. Suture care, drain follow-up, and nutrition protocol are monitored daily by the Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş team.

Abdominoplasty is not only an aesthetic procedure; it can reduce back pain related to abdominal wall weakness, correct posture disorders, and increase the daily life comfort of individuals. New body proportions can be obtained by planning with breast lift or liposuction in combined operations. Exercise plans specially prepared for each patient help to preserve the results for a long time.
