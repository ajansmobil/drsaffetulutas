Thigh Lift (Thigh Lift)

Thigh lift surgery is applied to reduce sagging and loose appearance in the inner thigh area. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş analyzes the patient's skin elasticity, fat accumulation, and general health status before the procedure. Deformations occurring in this area due to weight changes, pregnancy processes, or genetic structure are corrected with a combination of surgery and liposuction.

During surgery, excess skin is removed through incisions hidden in the groin line; if needed, additional tissue removal is performed with a vertical incision reaching close to the knees. The new contour obtained is fixed with dissolving sutures, and silicone gel treatments are recommended for the scars to fade over time. Dr. Ulutaş meticulously plans the surgery to reduce the risk of vascular and lymphatic complications.

Early mobilization after the operation supports blood circulation, while the use of a corset provides edema control. Patients speed up the healing process by starting light walks within the first two weeks. Regular position changes are recommended as long-term sitting or hanging legs from a height may increase edema.

Thigh lift is not only an aesthetic but also a functional solution that reduces skin irritation, friction, and hygiene problems. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş obtains a harmonious result in the entire lower body area by planning procedures such as butt lift or liposuction in the same session, if necessary. The new form is maintained with nutrition and sports recommendations given to patients.

For clients planning to have thigh lift surgery in Gaziantep, our clinic provides a safe experience with sterile operating room conditions and a friendly follow-up team. To achieve tighter, smoother, and aesthetic leg lines, you can plan a detailed examination with Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş.
