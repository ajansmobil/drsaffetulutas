Surgical Procedures

Reconstructive and plastic surgery are specialized areas that aim to eliminate both aesthetic and functional losses. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş provides a wide range of services from congenital anomalies to tissue loss after trauma, from tumor surgery to hand surgery. In operations performed in our clinic in Gaziantep, the priority is to preserve aesthetic integrity while increasing the patient's quality of life.

In interventions carried out using advanced surgical techniques and microscopic methods, sensitive procedures such as tissue transfers (flap surgery), scar revision, and skin grafts are applied. Dr. Ulutaş meticulously analyzes the biomechanics and anatomical requirements of each case and acts with the principle of the lowest scar and the highest functional gain.

Repair (reconstruction) after cancer surgery plays a key role, especially in breast reconstruction and skin tumor surgery. These procedures, which contribute to the psychological well-being of patients as well as their physical recovery, are supported by modern medical technologies. Our clinic offers uninterrupted follow-up and support to its patients until full recovery is achieved after surgery.
