Masseter Botox

Masseter botox is applied to reduce problems such as teeth grinding (bruxism) and the jawline looking wide. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş analyzes the strength of the jaw muscle, the level of tooth wear, and face proportions before the application. The goal is to thin the jawline while preserving chewing function.

Botox is injected into the masseter muscle from 3-5 points. The process takes a short time and comfort is increased by using topical anesthetic cream. Muscle strength begins to decrease within two weeks; pain caused by teeth grinding and headaches lighten. Aesthetically, the lower face looks thinner and more oval.

The effect of masseter botox lasts up to 6 months. A check is made in the second month in the first applications and an additional dose is given if necessary. With regular sessions, the strengthening of the muscle is prevented and the jawline becomes permanently elegant. Dr. Ulutaş works in cooperation with dentists to protect jaw joint and tooth health.

This application not only offers an aesthetic result; it reduces tooth fractures due to teeth grinding, jaw joint sounds, and jaw contractions. Sleep quality increases, and morning jaw fatigue disappears. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş supports profile integrity by combining masseter botox with jawline filler or jaw aesthetics.

For those who want to have masseter botox in Gaziantep, our clinic offers a safe experience with expert doctor control and personalized dose planning. You can create an appointment to alleviate your teeth grinding problem and thin your facial line.
