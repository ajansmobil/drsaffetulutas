Breast Aesthetic Surgery

Breast aesthetics involves breast augmentation, reduction, and lift operations in order to provide harmony in body proportions and increase the self-confidence of the person. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş takes into account factors such as chest cage width, shoulder line, waist thinness, and lifestyle when determining the ideal breast form for his patients. A detailed examination is performed before each operation; breast tissue is evaluated with ultrasound or mammography and safety is prioritized.

Specialized silicone implants or fat transfer options are offered in breast augmentation operations. When selecting an implant, parameters such as teardrop or round form, high or medium projection are determined according to the body characteristics of the patient. In breast reduction and mastopexy procedures, sagged tissues are removed, the nipple is moved to the point where it should be, and symmetry is provided. Dr. Ulutaş takes care that the scars remain in invisible areas as much as possible.

Pain control, the use of protective bras, and skin care protocols are applied in the post-operative process. Breast massage techniques accelerate tissue adaptation; healing is closely monitored with regular checks. While patients can return to desk jobs in 5-7 days, it is necessary to wait 6 weeks for sports and heavy activities. Specific instructions are offered to clients to prevent potential problems such as hardness or asymmetry in the breast tissue at every stage.

Breast aesthetics is not only a physical change; it is a process that strengthens the body perception of the person and liberates clothing choices. When correct planning is made, spine pain can decrease, and sports and daily activities become more comfortable. Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş focuses on obtaining results that preserve body integrity with combined operations (tummy tuck, liposuction, etc.).

Women and men who want to have breast aesthetics in Gaziantep can find solutions suitable for their personal goals in our clinic. Our team, which follows scientific developments in the field of aesthetic surgery, aims to keep the satisfaction rate high with safe operating room conditions and a careful follow-up system. You can contact Op. Dr. Saffet Ulutaş for breast aesthetics that harmonizes with your body form and looks natural.
